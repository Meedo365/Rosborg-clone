ROSBORG MØBLER


Rosborg Møbler has 1200 m2 of retail space in scenic
surroundings on the old main road between Aarhus
and Randers and a 250 m2 store in Aarhus - right next
to Magasin's car park.


We always strive to have an inviting exhibition, which
presents both the design classics that have been the
backbone of Rosborg's range since the start in 1966,
and a wide selection of new design furniture from
home and abroad
